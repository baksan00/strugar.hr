HISTORY

Alojz Strugar

If wanting to talk about a family
business with tradition, it would be
Strugar shoes

Tradition since 1967

This is where and this is how the story of Strugar shoes begins: 
In the very heart of the City of Zagreb, in its historical part
named 'Lower town', at Ilica the main street, a small salon was
situated back in 1967, which has neither spread out over time
spatially nor changed location, but the reputation about it has
spread beyond the City itself.

It is located in the courtyard beside the then satirical cabaret Jazavac (today's Kerempuh Theater), which was founded only a few years earlier, also
not far from the Cathedral itself, the main Square, from the most famous marketplace Dolac, and the historically significant Manduševac, the spot
where, according to the legend, a girl called Manda GRAB from the water well, after which event the City got its name. All in all, it is located where the
history of the City of Zagreb is written. In the same year the salon was founded, the city football club Dinamo, after excellent games, won the Fair Cities
Cup (later the UEFA Cup, today's UEFA Europa League). In this ambience and under the ownership of the Strugar family, the salon commenced its
activities and continued without closing or business interruptions until today. From that time until now, the salon is the symbol of an urban style linked
with the old tradition of the City of Zagreb.

In the year 1967, the entrepreneur Alojz Strugar started the shoemaking tradition of the Strugar family, investing his hard work, knowledge, proficiency,
and taste in fashion, which did not fall behind the leading fashion brands of that time.

Quality is success: the Strugar brand is
included in the list of the 500 best
shoes in the world by the World of
Shoes portal, which gathers all the
world's best shoe brands.

Long tradition in craft

It is one of the few stores left that exudes the spirit of
Zagreb's small crafts. The craft tradition in Zagreb has a
rich history dating back to the Middle Ages. The
beginning of economic development even then were
tradesmen-artisans, who had their unions and guilds,
which, adhering to strict rules, became the basis of
manufacturing production. Even though there is not as
many such crafts today, the centre of Zagreb still
resists. The Strugar salon is one of the few stores that
has survived thanks to the quality and the good image
it gained. The Strugar shoes created its own tradition
using the acquired inside knowledge and knowhow
made through the years.

We follow fashion trends also

In addition to the traditional features of our salon, on the basis of which the entire business was founded and profiled, the modern needs of clients
have also been considered and accepted, and new fashion trends were and are taken into account. As a consequence, the business was permanently
raised to new levels, and new fashion styles were added to our models.
In the long tradition of the Strugar family business, those shoes were often worn and appreciated by many distinguished artists, athletes and
politicians, such as Zlatko Dalić, Croatian famous football coach and former football player, and Jasmin Stavros, late famous singer and artist. Many of
customers, known or unknown, regularly visit the store.
