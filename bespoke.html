BESPOKE

In the Strugar salon only the
highest quality materials are used.

The Strugar shoes are made by hand, and their 'bespoke' feature makes them even more special. 'Bespoke' means that a client
modifies a shoe model already produced and in stock according to his own wishes. This possibility is of a great importance for
modern men who strive for authenticity in fashion expression. Grooms often decide to get the Strugar shoes, but so do everyone
who needs quality shoes for special occasions.

01

Measurements & Selection of style

In this phase a client comes with an idea of what kind of shoes he wants. The client's
foot is measured, and he chooses the model, i.e. the design and the material of shoes.
Also, some special wishes and needs are taken into account during this first step, as well as
some health problems that are important in the shoe making (e.g. wide feet or
prominent big toe bone etc.)

02

Shoe Making

After taking precise measurements, special attention is paid during the process of
production: firstly, the shoe last is made and the desired shoes are manufactured on
the basis of it. Then, leather is cut, and the lining is made, as well as all the accessories
required by the specific style of the shoes. After assembling the shoe parts, the upper
part of the shoes is completed and attached to the lower part.

03

Probe & Finalisation

The client is invited to the probe to check if the shoes fit him and if their appearance
suits him too. In this phase, there is the chance to make final changes in heel height,
toe shape, etc. With handmade shoes everything is dedicated to details. It is checked
whether the shoes have even a slightest pressure on the foot. Comfort is the key and
such process allows any adjustments to be made according to the client's wishes.
